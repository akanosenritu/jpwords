There are many train types in Japanese railway. The order given below is just a general guideline since it may vary depending on the train company.

* <Ruby t="ふつう">普通</Ruby> (ordinary) and <Ruby t="かくえき">各駅</Ruby> <Ruby t="ていしゃ">停車</Ruby> (lit. means "stops at every station") will stop at every station.
* <Ruby t="かいそく">快速</Ruby>　(rapid) will skip some stations, but less frequently than 急行 trains. Usually 快速 trains do not require a special ticket.
* <Ruby t="きゅうこう">急行</Ruby> (express) will skip many stations. Whether they require a special ticket depends on the train company. For JR trains, it is required.
* <Ruby t="とっきゅう">特急</Ruby> (special express) will skip most stations. Usually they require a special ticket.
